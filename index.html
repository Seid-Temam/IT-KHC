About Us

Kality Health Center, located in Woreda 07 of Akaki Kality Sub-City, is dedicated to delivering accessible, high-quality healthcare services to our growing community. As a trusted public health institution, we serve thousands of residents each year with compassion, professionalism, and a deep commitment to improving community well-being. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives timely and respectful care, regardless of their background. With a focus on prevention, education, and continuous service improvement, Kality Health Center stands as a pillar of health and hope for all who rely on us.

ourmission

We are dedicated to improving the health of our community through:

  • Providing accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services for all.
  • Promoting disease prevention and healthy living through education and outreach.
  • Ensuring maternal, child, and family health services are available and inclusive.
  • Strengthening community trust through compassionate, patient-centered care.
  • Collaborating with local and national health agencies to improve public health outcomes.
  • Empowering individuals with the knowledge and resources to manage their health.

ourvision

We strive to build a healthier future for our community by:

  • Becoming a leading example of community-based healthcare excellence.
  • Ensuring every resident has access to timely, affordable, and high-quality care.
  • Reducing preventable diseases through proactive education and outreach.
  • Creating a trusted, inclusive space where all individuals feel respected and cared for.
  • Embracing innovation and continuous improvement in healthcare delivery.
  • Promoting long-term wellness and sustainable public health solutions.

ourvalues

We are guided by the following principles in everything we do:

  • Compassion ‐ We treat every patient with kindness, dignity, and respect.
  • Equity ‐ We are committed to serving all individuals fairly, regardless of background or income.
  • Integrity ‐ We uphold the highest standards of honesty, ethics, and professionalism.
  • Accessibility ‐ We work to ensure that healthcare is available to everyone in our community.
  • Excellence ‐ We strive for the highest quality in care, service, and continuous improvement.
  • Collaboration ‐ We believe in teamwork among staff, patients, families, and community partners.

Frequently Asked Questions

We provide a range of essential health services including maternal and child health care, family planning, outpatient services, immunization, and health education & counseling.

Kality Health Center is open Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 5:30 PM for regular services. However, the emergency unit operates 24/7, including weekends and public holidays, to ensure urgent care is always available.

No appointment is needed for most services. Patients are served on a first-come, first-served basis during working hours. For some services like maternal care follow-ups, scheduled visits may be arranged.

Many services are provided free of charge or at a minimal cost, especially for children, mothers, and priority programs. Please ask at the reception for specific service details.

We are located in Woreda 07, Akaki Kality Sub-City, Addis Ababa — easily accessible from main roads and local transport lines.
